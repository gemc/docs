Description
^^^^^^^^^^^

The Jefferson Lab CLAS12 experiment sets the User defined variables as follows:

.. container:: lmydiv

   +---------------------------------------------------------------------+
   |          LUND Header                                                |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |   column     |                 quantity                             |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      1       |       **Number of particles**                        |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      2       |   Number of target nucleons                          |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      3       |    Number of target protons                          |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      4       |       Target Polarization                            |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      5       |      **Beam Polarization**                           |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      6       |       beam PID (electron=11, photon=22)              |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      7       |         beam energy                                  |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      8       |        target nucleon ID                             |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      9       |         process ID                                   |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      10      |        event weight/cross section                    |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+


.. container:: rmydiv


   +---------------------------------------------------------------------+
   |          LUND Particles                                             |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |   column     |                 quantity                             |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      1       |       **index**                                      |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      2       |         lifetime                                     |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      3       |    **type (1 is active)**                            |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      4       |     **particle ID**                                  |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      5       |       parent index                                   |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      6       |   index of the first daughter                        |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      7       |             **momentum x   [GeV]**                   |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      8       |             **momentum y   [GeV]**                   |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      9       |             **momentum z   [GeV]**                   |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      10      |            E                                         |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      11      |          mass                                        |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      12      |          **vertex x [cm]**                           |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      13      |          **vertex y [cm]**                           |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
   |      14      |          **vertex z [cm]**                           |
   +--------------+------------------------------------------------------+
